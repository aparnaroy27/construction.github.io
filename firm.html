ESTABLISHED IN BENGALURU, INDIA, SINCE 1980

Drem boat is a full service Construction firm. Our mission is to provide excellent service to our clients by listening to their dreams and desires and creating designs tailored to them. We believe that thoughtful, well detailed architecture can enrich lives.

COMPANY HISTORY

Established in 1994, Dream boat has realized continued growth over the past 30 years. We are known for our diverse project portfolio of exceptional residential architecture, award winning historic preservation projects, and cutting edge commercial designs. We remain committed to the utmost client service through a balance of technical knowledge and artistic expression.
