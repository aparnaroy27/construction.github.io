    Our Process

    Over the last 22 years we have refined our processes and have created an
    efficient work flow that gets our projects done on time and on budget.

    • 1 Client Introduction to BH+A (come meet the team!)
    • 2 Programming (information gathering and general discussions)
    • 3 Conceptual Design (Your desires and BH+A's creativity translated into sketches)
    • 4 Design Development (refining the design and getting the required approvals)
    • 5 Construction Drawings (Technical Drawings needed to fully explain the project)
    • 6 Bidding/Bid Review (Aiding in the Contractor Selection based on fit and price)
    • 7 Construction Observation (Ensuring the built project is true to the design)
    • 8 Project Review (Receiving feedback and applying it to the next job)
